CodeScript is a company originated in Brazil
and operating in the technology market since 2014

We are an innovative company with a skilled team and diverse backgrounds within the field of information technology. For almost a decade, we have been striving to turn our clients' dreams into reality. We are professionals who enjoy accepting challenges and turning ideas into accomplishments through technology, using various languages and methodologies. We operate in the fields of agribusiness, fleet management logistics, and Internet of Things (IoT).

We develop mobile applications, customized software tailored to clients' business rules, applications with embedded system interactions, and create scripts for IoT and automation. CodeScript also offers Outsourcing as a service, allowing your company to outsource various technology-related tasks, including specific software development, support for existing programs, team management, and more.

For your company

Innovate your business introducing technological solutions. CodeScript can provide services on app development, system modeling, team management and data security.

For your development team

If your company already works developing systems and applications, you can reinforce your team with our talented developers.

Mission

Our mission is to develop and improve processes and computational systems of our clients, providing with it the evolution of our collaborators and colleagues.

Vision

CodeScript aims to become a reference of technology for both our clients as well as our collaborators until the year 2025.

Values
