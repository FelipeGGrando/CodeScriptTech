#1 - EASY TRAVEL

The project we present is a solution for controlling the transportation of people, such as students and employees by bus companies. Through it, it is possible to customize the service for other purposes within this scope and offer a more efficient, safe, and personalized transportation experience. One of the main features of the project is the ability to create and manage routes through Google Maps, a popular and widely used mapping and navigation tool. Another important feature of the project is the management of passenger payments through QR code. With the use of technology, it is possible to make the payment process faster and more convenient for passengers, as well as reduce the risk of fraud and make the accounting process easier and more secure. The system also allows obtaining evaluations and feedback from passengers, which helps identify any problems and improve the service provided.

#2 - CODECROWND

The CodeCrownd Platform is a solution that uses blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies to create a secure and transparent environment where investors and innovators can connect and drive innovative projects. Through this platform, investors have the opportunity to contribute funds to promising projects, while innovators can publish their ideas and receive investments efficiently and reliably. Through this technology, all transactions are recorded in an immutable manner, creating a trustworthy environment for investors and innovators. Additionally, cryptocurrencies are used as a means of exchange and investment, eliminating traditional payment barriers and providing speed and efficiency in transactions. In summary, the platform brings together investors and innovators in a single innovation environment to make it possible to realize ideas using blockchain and cryptocurrencies.
